WHAT IS A CARBON FOOTPRINT?

A carbon footprint is the total amount of greenhouse gases that are generated by our daily actions. The most common greenhouse gases are water vapor, carbon dioxide, and methane. Greenhouse gases contribute to the greenhouse effect which warms the Earth's surface and leads to climate change. Decreasing our carbon footprint helps reduce climate change and global warming.
