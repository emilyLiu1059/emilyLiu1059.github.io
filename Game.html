TIME TO TEST WHAT YOU LEARNED WITH A GAME!

This game allows kids to learn about the steps that they can take to save and protect the environment. The simulation provides multiple scenarios where the player has to decide which action to take to be the most environmentally cautious (i.e. if they should use a reusable water bottle, or a plastic one). The scenarios range from walking around their house to turn off the tap, to choosing to use a reusable water bottle!
